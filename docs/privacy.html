Privacy Policy

Protecting your data and trust

We value your privacy and handle all personal information with transparency and care.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and project information when you contact us for consultations, support, or project inquiries. We may also collect technical information including IP addresses, device information, and browsing patterns for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Personal information is used to provide quotes, deliver contracted services, communicate project updates, and improve our solutions. We may use anonymized data to analyze service performance and enhance customer experience.

3. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with trusted partners who help us deliver our services (such as logistics or warranty providers) under strict confidentiality agreements. We retain information only as long as required to fulfill contractual or legal obligations.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights or to withdraw consent, contact us at info@nasam.co.ke. We will respond to all valid requests within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. While no method of transmission is completely secure, we continuously monitor and improve our safeguards.

6. Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in regulations or our services. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or direct notifications where appropriate.
